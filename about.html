About News Café

Your cozy corner for bold flavors and a welcoming vibe.

Our Story

News Café began as a dream to blend urban energy with warm café culture. From our first store to every table we serve today, our passion for rich coffee, vibrant dishes, and soulful music remains unchanged. We're more than a café — we’re a lifestyle.

The Concept

We bring together the essence of a contemporary eatery and a relaxed social space. Think artisan coffee, global bites, and a vibe that feels like home. At News Café, we don’t just serve food — we curate an experience.

Formula for Success

Great taste, warm service, and a fresh environment. Add a dash of community, a sprinkle of innovation, and a generous pour of love. That’s our recipe for success.

The Vibe

News Café is where laid-back charm meets everyday luxury. From soulful playlists to comfy seats, every detail is designed to elevate your café experience.
