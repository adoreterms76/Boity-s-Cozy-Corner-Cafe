Café Playlist Vibes: What We’re Listening to This Month

Published April 4, 2025 · by News Café

One thing that sets News Café apart? The *vibe*. And music is a big part of that. Whether you’re sipping a cappuccino on a slow morning or grabbing a late-evening mocktail with friends, our playlist sets the mood.

This month, we’ve curated a blend of soulful acoustics, jazzy lo-fi beats, and a few upbeat gems to keep your toes tapping. Here’s a little taste of what’s spinning through our speakers:

  • “Coffee in the Morning” – Miguel: Smooth, mellow, and perfectly brewed for your first sip of the day.
  • “Weightless” – Arlo Parks: Dreamy vocals that float through the café like morning mist.
  • “Night Trouble” – Petit Biscuit: Chillwave meets modern elegance—ideal for those laptop sessions.
  • “Sunday Vibes” – Masego & FKJ: Jazzy horns and a relaxed beat for that golden-hour glow.
  • “Good Days” – SZA: Because every latte deserves a little soul.

Want to take the café vibe with you? Follow our monthly playlists on Spotify—just search News Café Vibes and press play.

And hey, if you’ve got a favorite track that feels *so News Café*, let us know—we might just feature it next month!
